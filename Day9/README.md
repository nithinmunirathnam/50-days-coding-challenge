# 🍽️ Impact of Eating Out on Health & Lifestyle Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes how eating out frequency affects health indicators such as BMI, cholesterol levels, blood pressure, and overall health score. The dashboard is built using Power BI to uncover patterns between lifestyle habits and health outcomes.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze the impact of eating out frequency on health
* Identify relationships between diet, exercise, and health score
* Discover high-risk groups based on lifestyle patterns
* Provide actionable insights for healthier living

## 📁 Dataset Description

The dataset includes the following features:

* **Person_ID** – Unique identifier
* **Age** – Age of individuals (18–60)
* **Gender** – Male/Female
* **Occupation** – Student, Employee, Business, Freelancer
* **Eat_Out_Frequency** – Number of times eating out per week
* **Preferred_EatOut_Type** – FastFood, CasualDining, StreetFood, HealthyCafe
* **Daily_Calories** – Daily calorie intake
* **Exercise_Hours_Per_Week** – Weekly exercise duration
* **Sleep_Hours** – Daily sleep duration
* **BMI** – Body Mass Index
* **BloodPressure** – Health category
* **Cholesterol_Level** – Health category
* **Diabetes** – Yes/No
* **Health_Score** – Overall health rating

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)


## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 KPIs

* Average Eating Out Frequency
* Average Health Score
* Diabetes Percentage
* Hypertension Percentage

### 🔹 Visualizations

* Eating Out Frequency vs BMI
* Cholesterol Levels by Food Preference
* Calories vs Health Score (Scatter Plot)
* Eating Out vs Health Score by Age Group
* Distribution of Eating Out Types

### 🔹 Slicers

* Gender
* Age Group
* Occupation
* Eat-Out Frequency
* Eat-Out Type
* Health Risk


## 📊 Key Insights

* Frequent eating out is associated with higher BMI and poorer health outcomes
* Fast food consumption shows higher cholesterol levels
* Regular exercise improves health even with frequent eating out
* High calorie intake negatively impacts health score
* Poor sleep patterns are linked to lower health performance

## 📌 Conclusion

This project highlights how lifestyle habits, especially eating out, impact health. It emphasizes the importance of balanced diet, exercise, and proper sleep for maintaining good health.
